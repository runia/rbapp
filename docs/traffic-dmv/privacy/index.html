Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Summary: Traffic & DMV does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works fully offline, requires no account, contains no ads, and uses no tracking or analytics.

Information we collect

None. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal data. We do not create accounts and do not require sign-in.

Data stored on your device

Your selected state, chosen appearance (light/dark), and your in-app favorites are saved only on your device, in the app's local settings. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Analytics & tracking

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not use cookies or device identifiers.

Network use

The app's content is bundled and works offline. The only network requests occur if you tap an optional "official source" link, which opens that website in your browser. Those websites have their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Disclaimer

Traffic & DMV is an independent educational reference. It is not affiliated with any government agency, DMV, DOT, or court, and it does not provide legal advice.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
