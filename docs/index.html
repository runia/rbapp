How we build

The same four rules behind every app.

No fine print. These are the constraints we design within.

01

Offline by default

The whole library lives inside the app. Look up a fine on a back road, diagnose a car in a basement garage — no signal needed.

02

No accounts, ever

Open the app and start. There's nothing to sign up for and nothing to log in to — the tool is ready the moment you install it.

03

Private by design

No analytics, no servers, no tracking. What happens on your phone stays on your phone — that's the default, not a setting.

04

Focused, then maintained

Each app does one job and does it well, with content kept current against its official sources.
