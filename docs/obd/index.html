Capabilities

What it does

A complete native diagnostic tool — read your engine, look up trouble codes, and record your trips, all on device.

📟

Live dashboard

Real-time RPM, speed, coolant, throttle, MAF and 55+ Mode-01 PIDs. Three layouts, six themes, and a HUD windshield mode.

⚠️

Fault codes

Read confirmed, pending and permanent DTCs, clear them, and look up 12,135 codes with causes and symptoms in your language.

🩺

Deep diagnostics

Freeze frames, smog-readiness monitors, on-board monitor test results (Mode 06), VIN decoder and calibration IDs.

🗺️

Trip recorder

GPS route on the map, speed/RPM charts, 0-100 acceleration test, and export to PDF, CSV, GPX and KML.

🚙

Garage

Multiple vehicles with service reminders and a health timeline. First-class support for LADA, UAZ and GAZ.

🌍

Four languages

English, Русский, Deutsch, Español. One-time purchase — no ads, no in-app purchases.

🔒
